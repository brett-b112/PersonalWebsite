KU Audio Reader Database and Interface
The KU Audio Reader is a non-profit organization on the KU campus that provides audio materials such as publications, local newspapers, and books.

The KU Audio Reader had issues managing subscriptions because of the nature of the KU endowment which prohibited their ability to use a auto-renewal. As a result, they had issues tracking expiration dates and this provided a database and UI that could support this. The project was built with postgres, expressJS, react, and NodeJS.
